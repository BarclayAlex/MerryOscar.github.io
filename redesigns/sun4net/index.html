THE PRODUCT

We develop and build ground-mounted solar photovoltaic farms in the UK.

We buy land to put projects on, and provide financing for local communities to invest

We have successfully built and operated approximately 12 megawatts peak of similar projects in Germany over the past three years.

THE BENEFITS

Our solar farms are not permanent. Land can be re-used for other purposes if and when the farm is removed.

Our farms are silent running, with very little maintenance, are checked only once a year, and monitored remotely.

We have a very quick build time of a few weeks.

OUR PROMISE

We do not disrupt the land, spoil it, or take it out of use.

We provide clean, safe power to the local community for 20 years and more.

Our panels are unobtrusive as they are rarely higher than a person.
